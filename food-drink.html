    We serve all of our guests a delicious complimentary breakfast from 7:30 AM to 9:00 AM. A rotating menu of scrambled eggs, buttermilk pancakes, or Nancy’s famous french toast is paired with an assortment lighter of options to get you ready for a long day enjoying the mountains.

    • Fresh Fruit

    • Orange Juice & Milk
    • Organic Colorado Roasted Coffee

  • BEER & WINE

    Whats the next best thing to exploring in the mountains? Relaxing in our hot tub or by one of our roaring fires with a glass of vino in hand! The Bivvi Bar is open from 4:00 PM to 10:00 PM daily. We have three tasty brews on tap, an assortment of bottled beer, hard cider and red & white wines.

    Draft Beer- Hazed & Infused, Torpedo Extra IPA, Pabst Blue Ribbon

    Bottles- Fat Tire Amber, Blue Moon Belgium Wheat, Big B's Hard Cider

    White- Guenoc Chardonnay, Salmon Creek Pinot Grigio – CA – 2012

    Red- Santa Rita Pinot Noir, Salmon Creek Cabernet Sauvignon
