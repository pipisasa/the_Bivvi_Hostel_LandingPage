Правила заселения / выезда

У этого объекта размещения следующие условия и положения касательно даты заезда и отъезда

Заселение: 16:00

Отъезд: 11:00

  • Cancellations:
    • Cancellations 15 days or more from the reservation check-in date, forfeit full deposit amount.
    • Cancellations 14 days or less from the reservation check-in date, forfeit full amount of reservation.
    • Cancellations will not be refunded due to family emergencies, cancelled flights, closed roads, weather, etc. 
  • Payment:
    • 25% deposit of full reservation amount collected at time of booking.
      • Deposit is strictly Non-refundable.
    • Remaining 75% collected within 14 days of the reservation check-in date.
  • Reservation Date Changes:
    • No Fee for date changes 15 days or more from the reservation check-in date.
      • Subject to rate changes and availability.
    • Date changes 14 days or less from the reservation check-in date will be treated as a cancellation.
      • No Fee for date changes 14 days or less from the reservation check-in date in the Dorm Rooms during the Summer months (June 1st-September 30th).
        • Subject to rate changes and availability.
  • Policies:
    • No children under the age of 12.
    • Guest under the age of 18 are not permitted in the dorm rooms unless accompanied by an adult.
    • No dogs, cats, birds, spider monkeys, ring tailed mirror cats or any animals.
